-------------------------------------------------------------

Description: Print all the divisors of the given number in ascending order.

-------------------------------------------------------------

Input: In the only line of input you will be given number n. (n < 10000)

-------------------------------------------------------------

Output: Divisors of the number n, in ascending order, separated by a single space.

-------------------------------------------------------------

Examples: 

Input: 2
Output: 1 2

Input: 9322
Output: 1 2 59 79 118 158 4661 9322
