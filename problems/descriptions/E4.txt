-------------------------------------------------------------

Description: You are given two integers, a and b. Print a^b mod (10^9 + 7).

-------------------------------------------------------------

Input: In the only line of input you will be two integers separated by a single space. The first one will be a and the second one will be b. (0 < a < 3000, 0 < b < 35)

-------------------------------------------------------------

Output: A single integer, a^b mod (10^9 + 7).

-------------------------------------------------------------

Examples: 

Input: 9 3
Output: 729

Input: 2939 32
Output: 638857163
